Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is your candy taste approved by J.K Rowling?

A: Yes, we have worked extensively with Mrs. Rowling to ensure the quality of these delicious Harry Potter treats.

Q: Can I only buy products from your online store?

A: No, we actually have a central location at Harry Potter World in Orlando, Florida.

Q: Do you guys cater for parties?

A: We do cater for parties depending on locations. Go to our Contact page to reach us.

Q: Do you have any Harry Potter memorabilia that you sale or have on display in the store?

A: We do occasionally have auctions where we sell famous memorable items. However, we do have a lot of familiar items around if you visit our stores.

Q: How long have you been in business?

A: We were established right before Universal Studios bought the rights to begin building Harry Potter world in Orlando. We were then contacted by them and wanted to establish the store in the park.

Q: Are y'all Harry Potter fans?

A: Of course, how could we start a company that is dedicated to a small portion of the lore without absolutely loving the story.
